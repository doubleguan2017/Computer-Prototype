I suffered a torn ACL last year and I would highly recommend communicating with your professors. Mine were very understanding and aware of my circumstance which made it much easier if I was ever running late for class. I would also recommend finding a chair close the door and asking students not to use it, so you won't have to struggle with climbing over everyone to find a seat.
